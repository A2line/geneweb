Cleaning up

We are going to proceed to the cleaning up of the data base "$a".

Warning: this operation can take some seconds or minutes, depending on the cases. Be patient.

Push the button below to start the operation.

$h

For information, this operation corresponds to the following commands: 

     $$ cd $w
     $$ .$/gwu $a -o tmp.gw
     $$ mkdir old
     Under Unix:
       $$ rm -rf old/$a.gwb
       $$ mv $a.gwb old/.
     Under MSdos:
       $$ del old\$a.gwb\*.*
       $$ rmdir old\$a.gwb
       $$ move $a.gwb old\.
     $$ .$/gwc tmp.gw -o $a > comm.log
